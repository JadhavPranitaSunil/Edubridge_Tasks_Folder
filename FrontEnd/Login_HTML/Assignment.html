About the Company :

Color is such a fundamental part of the way we perceive the world that we often take it for granted. Think about it: From the youthful and vivid orange on someone's attire to the gray and gloomy sky above us, colors have the power to mold our perceptions of others and even the circumstances we find ourselves in. This is why one of the most powerful tools in a designer's arsenal is color. It can either make or break a design; it can be the determining factor in engaging viewers or sending them promptly on their way.

Incepted in the year 2017 at Jaipur (Rajasthan, India), we “The Guiding Star Corporation” are the leading manufacturer of a commendable and premium quality array of Acrylic Paintings, Canvas Painting, Oil Paintings and Wall Painting. We are a Sole Properiotorship company and manufacture the high-quality range of products in varied specifications to fulfill the diverse needs of our clients. Under the headship of “Mr. Shreyaa Singh” (Owner), we have been able to accomplish emerging requirements and demands of our customers.
